Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a team?

From the home screen, tap "Create Team" and select up to 5 drivers and 1 constructor within the $1,000 budget.

How do leagues work?

Create a league and share the join code with friends. League standings update automatically after each race.

What is an Ace?

You can designate one driver or constructor priced $200 or under as your Ace. Your Ace earns double base points.

How do contracts work?

Each driver is signed to a contract (default 3 races). When a contract expires, the driver leaves your team and you can sign a replacement.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile tab and tap "Delete Account", or visit our account deletion page.
