Privacy Policy

The Undercut
Effective Date: February 12, 2026

The Undercut ("we," "our," or "the App") is a fantasy Formula 1 game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Display name (chosen by you)
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by you or AI-generated)

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from the respective provider. We do not receive or store your Google or Apple password.

Game Data

We collect data related to your gameplay, including:

  • Fantasy team selections (drivers and constructors)
  • League memberships and league activity
  • Points scored and transaction history
  • Team names and avatar images

Device and Usage Data

We collect limited technical information for debugging purposes:

  • Device platform (iOS or Android)
  • App version
  • Error logs and crash reports (including stack traces)

Error logs are rate-limited (maximum 50 per session) and deduplicated to minimize data collection.

Data Stored Locally

The App stores certain data on your device using local storage, including your authentication state, team data, league data, news articles, and user preferences (such as dark mode and notification settings). This data remains on your device and is used to provide a faster, offline-capable experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Account creation and authentication Email, name, profile photo
Gameplay (teams, leagues, scoring) Game data, team selections
Avatar generation Team/league/user names (sent to Google Generative AI)
Bug fixing and app stability Error logs, device info, app version
Delivering F1 news content No personal data (RSS feeds are processed server-side)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

Google Firebase

We use Firebase for authentication, database (Firestore), file storage, and cloud functions. Your data is processed and stored on Google Cloud infrastructure. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

Google Generative AI (Gemini)

We use Google's Gemini AI to generate avatar images and summarize F1 news articles. When generating avatars, your team or league name is sent to Google's API. No other personal data is sent. See Google AI Terms.

Google Sign-In & Apple Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, your authentication is handled by the respective provider. We only receive your name and email. See Google Privacy Policy and Apple Privacy Policy.

OpenF1 API

We fetch publicly available Formula 1 race data from the OpenF1 API. No personal data is sent to this service.

Expo (EAS Updates)

We use Expo's update service to deliver app updates. See Expo Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your data is shared only with:

  • Other league members: Your display name, team name, avatar, and points are visible to members of leagues you join.
  • Service providers: The third-party services listed above, solely to operate the App.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Game data: Retained until you delete your account or leave a league.
  • Error logs: Retained indefinitely for debugging and app stability purposes. Error logs contain technical information and do not include personally identifiable information beyond your user ID.
  • Local data: Stored on your device until you sign out or uninstall the App.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the App (your profile, teams, and leagues).
  • Update your display name, profile photo, and notification preferences at any time.
  • Delete your account and associated data through the App's account settings.
  • Opt out of notifications through the App's settings.

To request a full export of your data, or if you have questions about your data, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Data at rest is encrypted by Google Cloud/Firebase.
  • Firestore security rules restrict access to authorized users only.
  • Authentication tokens are managed securely by Firebase Auth.
  • File uploads are restricted to images under 5 MB.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Advertising and Tracking

The App does not contain advertisements, tracking pixels, or commercial analytics SDKs. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
