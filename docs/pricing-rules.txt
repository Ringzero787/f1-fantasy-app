================================================================================
F1 FANTASY - PRICING & SCORING RULES (V3)
================================================================================

Currency: Dollars ($)

================================================================================
INITIAL PRICE CALCULATION
================================================================================

Formula:
  INITIAL_PRICE = (PREVIOUS_YEAR_TOTAL_POINTS / 24) * $10

Parameters:
  - RACES_PER_SEASON: 24
  - SPRINTS_PER_SEASON: 4
  - DOLLARS_PER_POINT: $10 per average point per race

Example:
  - Driver with 240 total points last season
  - Average per race: 240 / 24 = 10 points per race
  - Initial price: 10 * $10 = $100

================================================================================
DYNAMIC PRICE UPDATES
================================================================================

Prices update after each race based on a 5-race rolling average.

Formula:
  NEW_PRICE = ROLLING_AVG_POINTS * $10

Parameters:
  - ROLLING_WINDOW: 5 races
  - MAX_CHANGE_PER_RACE: $25 (increased volatility!)
  - MIN_PRICE: $3
  - MAX_PRICE: $500

================================================================================
SPRINT WEEKEND ADJUSTMENT
================================================================================

Sprint weekends weighted at 0.75 to normalize higher scoring potential.

  - Normal race max: 25 + 1 (fastest lap) = 26 points
  - Sprint weekend max: 25 + 1 + 8 (sprint) = 34 points
  - Weight of 0.75 normalizes this difference

================================================================================
CAPTAIN SYSTEM (Weekly Decision)
================================================================================

Each race weekend, choose 1 driver as Captain:
  - Captain scores 2x points
  - Must be selected before qualifying
  - Creates weekly engagement

Example:
  - Verstappen scores 25 points as Captain = 50 fantasy points
  - Same race without Captain = 25 fantasy points

================================================================================
STALE ROSTER PENALTY (Encourages Active Play)
================================================================================

Players who don't make transfers are penalized:

  - THRESHOLD: 5 races without a transfer
  - PENALTY: -5 points per race after threshold
  - MAX PENALTY: -95 points (19 races Ã— -5)

Example:
  - No transfers all season = -95 points penalty
  - Transfer once every 5 races = no penalty

================================================================================
TRANSFER BONUSES (Rewards Smart Moves)
================================================================================

HOT HAND BONUS:
  - New transfer scores 15+ points: +10 bonus
  - New transfer finishes on podium: +15 bonus
  - Only applies to the race immediately after transfer

VALUE CAPTURE BONUS:
  - Sell driver for profit: +5 points per $10 profit
  - Example: Buy at $100, sell at $150 = +25 bonus points

================================================================================
POINTS REFERENCE
================================================================================

Race Points (Top 10):
  1st: 25    6th: 8
  2nd: 18    7th: 6
  3rd: 15    8th: 4
  4th: 12    9th: 2
  5th: 10   10th: 1

Sprint Points (Top 8):
  1st: 8     5th: 4
  2nd: 7     6th: 3
  3rd: 6     7th: 2
  4th: 5     8th: 1

Bonus Points:
  - Fastest Lap: +1 (if finished in top 10)
  - Position Gained: +1 per position (grid vs finish)

================================================================================
TIER CLASSIFICATION
================================================================================

  A-TIER: Price > $200
    - Larger price swings (+/- up to $25 per race)
    - Premium drivers, higher risk/reward

  B-TIER: Price <= $200
    - Moderate price swings
    - Budget-friendly options

================================================================================
TEAM BUDGET
================================================================================

  - Starting Budget: $1,000
  - Team Composition: 5 drivers + 1 constructor
  - Budget must cover all selections
